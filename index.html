About

Me

I have more than 10 years of professional experience in web development. Building a fast, scalable, and responsive website has always been my passion.

For the past 2.5 years I worked for Verizon as a Senior Software Engineer. I was in the Verizon Lab under Internet Service Team, where we build innovate products to support company's various needs. I had an opportunity to use some of the latest technologies like AngularJS and React on the projects. It was such a rewarding experience to know that the products I worked on will be viewed and used by millions of Verizon customers in US.

I joined Google's Brand Studio team in May of 2013 as a Web Developer, I have worked with many talented developers in the team. I was assigned to the Google Social/Geo and .org pod, and focused on the creation and maintenance of Google's various marketing sites.

Prior to Google I worked for E*TRADE. I was in a mobile app team and we built a web based application for tablets. I was responsible for writing a clean and semantic front-end code across different devices. The app was released in March 2013, and was available to download on iTunes and Google Play.

When not coding I enjoy playing tennis and hanging out with friends. I am a big fan of Roger Federer, and still believe he can win another Grand Slam title. I am also a music lover, and enjoy playing piano and guitar in my spare time.
